AenoxDocs Data Deletion

AenoxDocs does not require an account or sign-in. The app stores imported PDFs, folders, preferences, and app-managed library data locally on your device.

Delete local app data

You can delete local AenoxDocs data by:

  • deleting PDFs inside AenoxDocs
  • deleting folders inside AenoxDocs
  • clearing app data from Android system settings
  • uninstalling AenoxDocs from your device

Subscriptions and billing records

AenoxDocs Pro subscriptions are handled by Google Play Billing. You can manage or cancel subscriptions through Google Play. Google may retain billing and purchase records under its own policies.

Crash diagnostics

AenoxDocs uses Sentry for crash and error diagnostics. Crash diagnostic data may be retained by Sentry according to its own retention practices.

Request help

If you have a deletion-related question or privacy request, contact: aenoxstudio@gmail.com.

Please include "AenoxDocs data deletion request" in the subject line.
